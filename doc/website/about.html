About caRuby

caRuby is developed by and for cancer centers. The intent of caRuby is to reduce the barriers to entry for adopting caBIG tools. You are invited to contribute to this effort with advice, problem reports or code. Post suggestions and questions in the Forum. See the Modification FAQ page for more information about how to modify the caRuby code.
